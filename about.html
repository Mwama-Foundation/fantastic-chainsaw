Who We Are

Who Is Mwama Foundation?

Mwama Foundation is a Zambian registered Non-Governmental Organization that promotes uplifting the quality of life of women through life changing empowerment programs. We believe that anyone can become self-sustaining. With support they can be empowered to identify and grasp opportunities. And that education and opportunity are the keys to unlocking women’s potential.

Our Vision & Mission

To provide skills and support to unemployed Zambians to remove inequalities caused by multidimensional poverty. Empowering the unemployed Zambians to become self-sustaining financially by giving them support and guidance to create viable businesses.

How We Are Making A Difference

Imilimo Project

Imilimo is a social enterprise program that aims to empower female entrepreneurs with skills and resources required to start a business. Through the Comprehensive Business Development Programme (CBDP), a multi-approach training program, we work with unemployed mothers / women to help them become successful traders. The women will acquire life skills, business skills and computer skills. And have access to coaching, mentorship and a support network of like-minded women. And they receive a qualification and become business owners

Elevation Hub For Skills Development

About 60 % of women too often are pressed into choices that worsen female poverty. Unemployment rate is generally higher among women, and lack of skills place them at great risk. This has led many women opting for risky ways to earn some money running risks like HIV/AIDS infections and early pregnancies.Computer skills training offers them an opportunity to acquire skills to become competitive in the labor market.

Dignity Packs distribution Program

Lack of sanitary pads is attributed as one of the main reasons for the girl child dropout rate between grades 1-9. Our project offers a holistic approach to MHM. Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) is a vital aspect of every woman’s life and it defines the practice of being able to manage your period safely. We provide dignity kits for free to ensure women and girls can manage their period safely. We aim to provide access to private and safe places to change sanitary products with water sanitation and hygiene and disposal facilities. And education on menstruation that will enable girls to better understand the natural changes their bodies go through.
