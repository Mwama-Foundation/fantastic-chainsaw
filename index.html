Who We Are

Mwama Foundation is a Zambian registered Non-Governmental Organization that promotes uplifting the quality of life for people through life changing empowerment programs. We provide skills and support to unemployed Zambians to remove inequalities caused by multidimensional poverty.

  • Learn more

What we do

Despite Zambia’s economic successes and high growth rates, the country displays high inequalities in wealth distribution and quality of life. Extreme rural poverty and high unemployment levels remain significant problems. Currently, close to 64% of Zambians live under $2 a day and the majority of those who earn more barely make ends-meet. Indeed, over 40% of them are considered to live in extreme poverty (under $1.25 a day)

We cannot wait for the system to find solutions while our people are in despair. It is for this reason we established the Mwama Foundation. Our programs provide unemployed Zambians with an alternative to formal employment by empowering them to become self-employed business people, and well-being.
